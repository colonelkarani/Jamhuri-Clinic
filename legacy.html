The Beginning

In the heart of Thika, where the coffee farms meet the bustling town, a vision was born in 2002. Dr. George Maingi, fresh from his specialized training in orthopaedics, arrived with a singular mission: to bring world-class bone and joint care to the people of Central Kenya.

What started as a modest one-room clinic on Thika's main street, equipped with basic capabilities and Dr. Maingi's unwavering dedication, would grow to become the region's most trusted orthopaedic center.

Dr. Maingi's approach was revolutionary for its time. He combined cutting-edge medical expertise with genuine compassion, spending hours explaining procedures to worried families, making house calls for immobile elderly patients, and never turning away anyone who couldn't pay immediately.

By 2009, the clinic had outgrown its original space. The first expansion added a dedicated physiotherapy unit, marking the beginning of our comprehensive care philosophy that treats not just the injury, but the whole person.

Dr. George Maingi

Founder & Chief Orthopaedist

"When I started this clinic in 2002, my vision was simple: every person in Thika and beyond deserves access to world-class orthopaedic care. I wanted to create a place where medical excellence meets human compassion, where technology serves healing, and where every patient feels like family."

Dr. Maingi has undergone specialized training in the Singapore, Turkey, UAE and Malaysia. He has performed over 8,000 procedures and continues to mentor the next generation of orthopaedic surgeons.

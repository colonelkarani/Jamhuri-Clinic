Advanced Arthritis Management

Our arthritis program combines cutting-edge pharmacological treatments with surgical interventions when necessary. We specialize in biologics, viscosity supplementation and cartilage preservation techniques.

Individualized treatment plans may include weight management strategies, physical therapy protocols, and minimally invasive joint preservation procedures.

Complex Trauma Reconstruction

Our trauma specialists provide 24/7 emergency orthopaedic care using the latest fixation technologies. We specialize in polytrauma management, periarticular fractures and limb salvage procedures.

Each case receives a customized rehabilitation roadmap from acute treatment through final recovery stages.

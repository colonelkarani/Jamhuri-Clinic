Enter or Update Health Information

Click on any health metric card (weight, blood pressure, heart rate, etc.) to add new measurements or update existing ones. Simply enter your values and the date, then save.

2

View and Track Health Trends

Navigate to the "Trends" or "History" section to see visual charts and graphs. Use the date range selector to view specific time periods and identify patterns in your health data.

3

Schedule or View Appointments

Go to the "Appointments" section to add upcoming doctor visits, view your appointment history, and set reminders. You can also add notes about each appointment.

4

Download/Export Health Data

Click the "Export" button in the top navigation or settings menu. Choose your preferred format (PDF, CSV, or Excel) and date range to download your complete health records.

Vitals

Visual graphs and charts that track changes in your health metrics over time. Identify patterns, monitor progress toward health goals, and spot any concerning trends early.

Appointments

Manage and view all your medical visits in one place. See upcoming appointments, review past visits, and add notes or documentation related to each appointment.

Medications

Track your current medications, dosages, and schedules. Set up reminders to take medications on time and log any side effects or notes.

Download your health records in various formats. Perfect for sharing with healthcare providers or keeping personal backups of your health information.

Alerts & Reminders

Automatic notifications for upcoming appointments, medication times, and when health metrics fall outside your normal ranges. Stay on top of your health management effortlessly.

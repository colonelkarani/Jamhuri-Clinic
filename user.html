Welcome to your Health Tracker Dashboard

Your personal, centralized place to monitor and manage all your health information—quickly, securely, and in one glance.

How to Use the Dashboard

  1. Add / update data: click the blue “＋” button on any card (Vitals, Weight, BP, etc.) and save.
  2. Track trends: open the “Trends” tab to see auto-generated charts (7 d, 30 d, 90 d views).
  3. Appointments: hit “Schedule” to add a new entry; toggle calendar view for upcoming visits.
  4. Export records: choose “Download” (CSV or PDF) in the Export card—data is anonymized locally.

Dashboard Sections at a Glance

  • Vitals: latest readings (weight, BP, heart rate, temperature).
  • Trends / History: interactive graphs showing changes over time.
  • Appointments: upcoming & past visits with provider details.
  • Export: one-click download of your entire record.
  • Alerts: color-coded reminders (e.g., overdue BP check).

Need Help?

Email support at support@myhealth.app or use the in-app “Report Issue” link at the bottom of the sidebar.

Tips for Best Results

  • Update vitals at least once a week—consistent data powers accurate trends.
  • Log measurements at the same time of day to reduce natural variation.
  • Review trend graphs monthly to spot gradual changes early.
  • Enable browser notifications so you never miss an appointment or alert.

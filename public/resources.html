Osteoarthritis

Wear-and-tear joint disease • Knees, hips, hands

Key Signs

  • Joint stiffness < 30 min in the morning
  • Pain that worsens with activity, eases with rest
  • Grating or crackling sound on movement
  • Mild swelling around the joint
Chair Stand: Sit-to-stand 10 reps, 2×/day. Builds quad strength without stressing cartilage.
Heel-to-Butt Stretch: Hold 30 s each leg to keep knees flexible.
Top free foods: Oily fish (sardines), turmeric-ginger tea, leafy greens. Download our 7-day OA meal planner PDF—no email required.
NHS-approved apps: MyOA (iOS/Android), ESCAPE-pain videos on YouTube. Free knee-brace sizing chart printable.

Low Back Pain

Mechanical pain • 80 % resolve in 6 weeks

When to Self-Care vs Seek Help

  • Self-care: Achy, improves with walking
  • Doctor today: Numb groin, leg weakness, fever
  • Red-flag: History of cancer or steroids
Cat-Camel: 10 slow reps on all-fours resets facet joints.
Press-up: 5 prone press-ups relieve disc pressure instantly.
Best free positions: Side-sleep with pillow between knees, or back-sleep with rolled towel under lumbar curve.
Zero-cost fix: Raise monitor to eye-level with books; every 25 min stand & roll shoulders 10×.

Rotator-Cuff Pain

Shoulder impingement • Over-use injury

Spot the Pattern

  • Pain when lifting arm sideways 60-120°
  • Night pain lying on affected shoulder
  • Weakness with overhead tasks
External rotation at 90°: Use bicycle-tube or 1-litre water bottle. 3×15 reps daily.
90-second reset: Roll shoulders back & down, tuck chin, hold 30 s. Repeat every hour.
Free cold pack: Frozen peas 15 min. Heat later if > 5 days old injury.

Plantar Fasciitis

Heel-spur syndrome • First-step pain

Classic Story

  • Sharp pain first step out of bed
  • Eases after 10 min walking
  • Returns after long standing
Towel stretch: Loop towel around ball of foot, pull toes toward you 30 s before standing up.
Twist test: If you can wring your sneaker like a cloth, it's too soft. Swap for firmer heel counter.
Free roller: Frozen water bottle or broomstick, roll arch 2 min morning & evening.

Carpal Tunnel

Median nerve compression • Tingling fingers

Quick Triage

  • Tingling thumb, index, middle finger
  • Night pain relieved by shaking hand
  • Weakness dropping small objects
Median nerve glide: Make “stop” sign, then slowly open fingers & extend wrist 10 reps each hour.
Zero-cost setup: Keyboard flat, elbows 90°, wrist neutral—use folded towel as palm rest.
Cardboard splint: Cut cereal box to L-shape, tape around wrist at bedtime to keep neutral.
